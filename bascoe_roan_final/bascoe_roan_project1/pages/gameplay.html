The Game Play

Battle System

Final Fantasy X uses a Conditional Turn-Based Battle (CTB) system. While this system still requires strategy to defeat your opponents without giving them more opportinities to defeat you, you don't have to worry about being rushed since there is no timer for how fast you have to make a decision. Certain characters get more turns based on their agility and by the use of spells or abilites, such as Haste.

Sphere Grid System

Players use the Sphere Gris system to grow the characters in their party. After each successful battle, your characters gain abilty points and different types of spheres. Players then use these to enhance their characters abilites in areas such as attacks skills, defense skills, black magic, and white magic.

Enemies

The story of Final Fantasy X has two reoccuring enemies throughout the story. These enemies are Sin, the final boss, and Maester Seymour Guado, a deranged influential figure in Spira that uses his power for his own sinister ends.
