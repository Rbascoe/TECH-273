The Story

Listen to my story. This... may be our last chance. - Tidus



Summary

Final Fantasy X follows the story of Tidus, a star blitzball player with the Zanarkand Abes. One fateful night, while playing in a blitzball match, Tidus' world is shattered when a supernatural monstrous entity known as "Sin" attacks Zanarkand. Amid the destruction, Tidus crosses paths with Auron, a friend of Tidus' father Jecht, whom Tidus had known during his childhood.

Auron gives Tidus a sword and instructs him to combat the monsters, referred to as "fiends," that are spawned by Sin. While Tidus and Auron engage these fiends, their world starts to be drawn closer to Sin. As they approach Sin, Auron seemingly communicates with it. He then turns to Tidus and says, "This is your story." Tidus is seemingly absorbed by Sin and is mysteriously transported 1,000 years into the future to the world of Spira, where his journey begins.



Author: Roan Bascoe
